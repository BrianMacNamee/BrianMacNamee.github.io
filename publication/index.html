Writing

Brian writes extensively on topics surrounding artificial intelligence and machine learning, ranging from technical explanations about how things work to broader societal topics about how we will leave in an ever more automated world. In 2015 Brian co-authored the textbook Fundamentals of Machine Learning for Predictive Data Analytics: Algorithms, worked Examples, and Case Studies published by MIT Press. This textbook is translated into 4 languages and widely adopted at top universities around the world.
